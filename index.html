What we do

James Clark Consulting has a clear purpose. We support businesses to deliver profitable growth.
Our aim is to

  • Provide tailored assistance to growth through sales, marketing, and product development
  • Support your team to make a sustainable difference in your business
  • Engage with Business Services organisations to shape and develop your commercial offering
  • Achieve profitable results for your people using our services

Deep and longstanding experience has taught us that action is the way to bring about change and making things happen in a business is all about deeds. The business itself, usually knows what is missing or needs to be done but often doesn't have the skilled resource or isn’t sure how to start. We work with in-house teams to make a tangible and sustainable difference to future business performance. We help you develop your thinking and devise successful strategies, ensuring that your teams understand them, are fully on board with them and know how to take them forward. We adapt and refine our approach to take account of the specific needs of your business. Consultancies tend to define their benefit or offer solely based on their knowledge; either of a specific niche, or the experience of their consultants. We recognise the value of thinking but we believe that what is fundamental, is the doing. We want to make things happen for clients by producing tangible, profitable outputs that can be seen on the balance sheet.

About us

James Clark Consulting is a boutique business consultancy that works with our clients to provide in-depth support to help find and fix their issues. Our small core team is supported by regular independent consultants we have chosen for their practical, hands-on experience as well as their wide-ranging business knowledge.

There is no ‘one size fits all’ approach. Our flexible approach means that we match our consultants to your business, so you’re working with the people who have the specific skills-sets and culturally 'get' your business. Crucially, we involve your teams, rather than leaving them on the subs bench, so we equip them with the knowledge and capability of running with the outputs. We are not authors of little-read papers preaching theories that will derive benefits in never-never land.

Our services

Business proposition

Which way to go, how will we resource it and will it work? Work rate and intuition is always a start but getting the direction of travel established and signed off by the business is the sort of initiative that points the way to the runway towards a bigger more profitable future. We can help you work these things through to both produce, and see through, business plans that will remove doubt and negativity.

Project management

Successful new products and ranges will add profitable growth to your business...unless... they end up as diversionary pet projects that suck up time and resource and send your business head over handlebars. Moving to action on new products and away from pondering requires market awareness, focus and discipline. New initiatives need to be held to account and James Clark Consulting can support you working at pace to deliver real and lasting results.

Sales improvement

Whether you are a start up business or a large organisation in the business services sector, sales can always be better. Attracting new prospective clients and converting them into customers who promote your business won't happen unless your team is disciplined and target focused. How much did the sales team bring in last quarter? And how much did they predict? Enough said.
